**Step 3: Findings Review & Risk Communication**


**Purpose**

Translate the assessment findings clearly to the client and assist in client understanding of security concerns.

This step's goal is the successfully bridge the gap between the tecnical assessment and decision making along with establishing additional concerns from the client based on the findings.


**Approach**

How the purpose is achieved is through conducting a consultation with the client post security assessment and run through the findings with the client to establish that the client has a understanding of all found security concerns.

Process:
- Review the assessement findings results with the client
- Translate technical security gaps into real-world business impact
- Communicate the risk prioritization ratings that will published in the final delivery findings report
- Clarify any technical issues and validate client understanding

Key Focus:
- Communication of findings
- Establish risk prioritization
- Validate client understanding


**Output**
Post consultation results in a final understanding to review the assessment findings and have all needed information to publish the final delivery report for the client.
