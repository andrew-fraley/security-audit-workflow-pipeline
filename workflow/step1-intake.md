**Step 1: Intake**

**Purpose**

- Define scope of the project
- Intake questionnaire and consultation interview
- Establish the business and data operations of the client
- Get an understanding of the business risk context based on interview and questionnaire

**Key Activities**

- Client questionnaire
- Intake interview
- Business and data profiling
- Scope definition

**Input**

Essential Information:

- Business profile and operations
- Data types and sensitivity levels
- Systems, tools, and processes
- User access and roles
- Vendors and thrid parties
- Existing security controls
- Prior incidents (if any)
- Asset inventory
- Compliance requirements
- Goals and concerns

**Output**

- Defined assessment scope
- Intial risk context
- Identified critical assets and business operations
- Adequately defined business profile and processes

**Crucial**

Poor intake = inaccurate assessment = useless results
